About Cinemora

At Cinemora, movies aren’t just entertainment—they’re experiences worth sharing. From timeless classics to the newest premieres, every film deserves its place on the reel.

Our Story

Cinemora started as a simple idea during a late-night movie marathon: what if ranking films was as fun as watching them? We built a space where anyone can discover great movies, rate them their way, and see how they stack up against the rest.

Our Mission

  • Give movie fans a voice — your ratings and lists matter.
  • Make ranking simple & fun — clean design, zero friction.
  • Bring the film community closer — discover together, debate together.

How It Works

  1. Discover: Browse by genre, year, or popularity.
  2. Rank: Rate films with a tap and build your top lists.
  3. Compare: See how your taste stacks up with the community.

Our Community

Whether you’re a casual watcher or a passionate critic, Cinemora welcomes you. Share your lists, champion underrated gems, and join the conversation—because at Cinemora, your movies, your rankings.
